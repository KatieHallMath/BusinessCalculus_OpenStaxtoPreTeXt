We should also point out that in the graphs of \(f(x)=1/ (x-a)^n ,\) points on the graph having \(x\)-coordinates very near to \(a \) are very close to the vertical line \(x=a.\) That is, as \(x\) approaches \(a \text{,}\) the points on the graph of \(f(x)\) are closer to the line \(x=a.\) The line \(x=a\) is called a vertical asymptote of the graph.
