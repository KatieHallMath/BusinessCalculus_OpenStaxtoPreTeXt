Definition 2.53.

Let \(f(x)\) be a function. If any of the following conditions hold, then the line \(x=a\) is a vertical asymptote of \(f(x).\)

\begin{align*} \lim_{x\to a^{-} }f(x) \amp = \amp +\infty \text{ or } "-\infty" \\ \lim_{x\to a^{+}}f(x) \amp = \amp +\infty \text{ or } "-\infty" \\ \amp \text{ or } \amp \\ \lim_{x \to a} f(x) \amp =\amp +\infty \text{ or } "-\infty" \end{align*}
