Item 2.
If the values of \(f(x)\) decrease without bound as the values of \(x\) (where \(x \gt a \)). approach the number \(a \text{,}\) then we say that the limit as \(x\) approaches \(a \) from the right is negative infinity and we write
\begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to a^{+}}f(x)=-\infty. \end{equation*}
