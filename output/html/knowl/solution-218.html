Solution 3.154.1.

Use the derivative of the natural exponential function, the quotient rule, and the chain rule.

\begin{align*} y'\amp=\frac{(e^{x^2}\cdot 2)x\cdot x- 1\cdot e^{x^2}}{x^2}\amp \amp\text{ Apply the quotient rule. }\\ \amp =\frac{e^{x^2}(2x^2- 1)}{x^2}\amp \amp\text{ Simplify. } \end{align*}
