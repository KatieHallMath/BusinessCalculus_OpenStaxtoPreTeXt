The zeros of this function can be found by solving the equation \(−1.04p^2+ 26 p= 0 .\) When we factor the quadratic expression, we get \(p( −1.04 p+ 26 )= 0 .\) The solutions to this equation are given by \(p= 0 , 25 .\) For these values of \(p,\) the revenue is zero. When \(p=\$ 0 ,\) the revenue is zero because the company is giving away its merchandise for free. When \(p=\$ 25 ,\) the revenue is zero because the price is too high, and no one will buy any items.
