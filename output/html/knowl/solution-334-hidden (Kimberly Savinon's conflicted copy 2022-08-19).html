First rewrite the problem using a rational exponent:

\begin{equation*} \int e^x\sqrt{1+e^x}dx=\int e^x(1+e^x)^{1/2}dx \end{equation*}

Using substitution, chose \(u=1+e^x\text{.}\) Then, \(du=e^x dx\text{.}\) We have (FigureÂ 5.92)

\begin{equation*} \int u^{1/2}du=\frac{u^{3/2}}{3/2}+C=\frac{2}{3}u^{3/2}+C=\frac{2}{3}(1+e^x)^{3/2}+C. \end{equation*}
