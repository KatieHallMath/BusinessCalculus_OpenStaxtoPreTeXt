Since midnight is \(3\) hours past \(9\) p.m., we want to compute \(T'(3).\) Refer to (3.1.3).

\begin{align*} T'(3)\amp=\lim_{t\to 3}\frac{T(t)-T(3)}{t-3}\amp\amp\text{ Apply the definition. }\\ \amp=\lim_{t\to 3}\frac{0.4t^2-4t+70-61.6}{t-3}\amp\amp\text{ Substitute } T(t)=0.4t^2-4t+70 \text{ and }T(3)=61.6.\\ \amp=\lim_{t\to 3}\frac{0.4t^2-4t+8.4}{t-3}\amp\amp\text{ Simplify. }\\ \amp=\lim_{t\to 3}\frac{0.4(t-3)(t-7)}{t-3}\amp\amp \text{Factor.}\\ \amp=\lim_{t\to 3}0.4(t-7)\amp\amp\text{ Cancel. }\\ \amp=-1.6\amp\text{ Evaluate the limit. } \end{align*}
