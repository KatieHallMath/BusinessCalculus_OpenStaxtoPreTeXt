Item 1.
Consider the graph in Figure 1.117 of the function \(y=\sin\,x+\text{ cos }\,x.\) Describe its overall shape. Is it periodic? How do you know? Using a graphing calculator or other graphing device, estimate the \(x\)- and \(y\)-values of the maximum point for the graph (the first such point where \(x\) \gt 0). It may be helpful to express the \(x\)-value as a multiple of \pi.
