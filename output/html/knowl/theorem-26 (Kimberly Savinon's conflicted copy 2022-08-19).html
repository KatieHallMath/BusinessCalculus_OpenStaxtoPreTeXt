Theorem 4.81. Second Derivative Test.

Suppose \(f'(c)=0,f''\) is continuous over an interval containing \(c.\)

  1. If \(f''(c)\gt 0,\) then \(f\) has a local minimum at \(c.\)

  2. If \(f''(c)\lt 0,\) then \(f\) has a local maximum at \(c.\)

  3. If \(f''(c)=0,\) then the test is inconclusive.
