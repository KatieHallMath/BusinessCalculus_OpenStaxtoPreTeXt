Theorem 5.47. Comparison Theorem.

  1. If \(f(x)\geq 0\) for \(a\leq x\leq b,\) then

    \begin{equation*} \displaystyle{\int_{a}^{b} f(x)dx} \geq 0. \end{equation*}

  2. If \(f(x)\geq g(x)\) for \(a\leq x\leq b,\) then

    \begin{equation*} \displaystyle{\int_{a}^{b} f(x)dx} \geq \displaystyle{\int_{a}^{b} g(x)dx} . \end{equation*}

  3. If \(m\) and \(M\) are constants such that \(m\leq f(x)\leq M\) for \(a\leq x\leq b,\) then

    \begin{align*} m(b-a)\amp \leq \displaystyle{\int_{a}^{b} f(x)dx} \\ \amp \leq M(b-a). \end{align*}
