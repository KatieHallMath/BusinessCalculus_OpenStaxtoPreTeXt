We now know that the slope of the tangent line is \(-\frac{1}{4}.\) To find the equation of the tangent line, we also need a point on the line. We know that \(f(2)=\frac{1}{2}.\) Since the tangent line passes through the point \((2,\frac{1}{2})\) we can use the point-slope equation of a line to find the equation of the tangent line.
