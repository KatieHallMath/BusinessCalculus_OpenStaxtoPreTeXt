Definition 5.21.

Let \(f(x)\) be defined on a closed interval \([a,b]\) and let \(P\) be a regular partition of \([a,b].\) Let \(\Delta x \) be the width of each subinterval \([x_{i-1} ,x_i]\) and for each \(i\text{,}\) let \(x_i^* \) be any point in \([x_{i-1} ,x_i].\) A Riemann sum is defined for \(f(x)\) as

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle{ \sum_{i=1}^{n} f(x_i^* )\Delta x }. \end{equation*}
