Item 2.

To find the domain of \(f\text{,}\) we need \(4 –x^2 \geq 0 \text{.}\) Or, \(4 \geq x^2 \) Or \(x^2 \leq 4 \text{,}\) the solution to which is \(– 2 \leq x\leq 2 \text{.}\) Therefore, the domain is \({x|– 2 \leq x\leq 2 } \text{.}\) If \(– 2 \leq x\leq 2 \text{,}\) then \(0 \leq 4 –x^2 \leq 4 \text{.}\) Therefore, \(0 \leq \sqrt{ 4 –x^2 }\leq 2 \) and the range of \(f\) is \({y| 0 \leq x\leq 2 }\text{.}\)
